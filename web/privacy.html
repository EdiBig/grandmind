Introduction

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Grandpoint Ltd ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the Kinesa fitness and wellness tracking application ("App," "Service").

By using Kinesa, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Legal Basis

We process your data under:

  • UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR)
  • EU General Data Protection Regulation (EU GDPR)
  • Data Protection Act 2018 (UK)
  • Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR)

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Email address (required)
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Fitness and Health Data (voluntarily provided by you):

  • Workout details (exercises, sets, reps, weight, duration)
  • Habit tracking (daily check-ins, custom habits)
  • Mood and energy levels (1-5 ratings, context tags)
  • Nutrition logs (meals, photos, calorie/macro data)
  • Sleep logs (hours, quality ratings)
  • Progress photos
  • Body measurements
  • Goals and targets
  • Free-text notes and comments

Payment Information:

  • Payment is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play)
  • We do not store your credit card or payment details
  • We receive transaction confirmations only (subscription status, purchase date)

1.2 Information from Third-Party Integrations

Wearable Devices and Health Platforms (with your explicit permission):

  • Apple Health (iOS): Steps, sleep, workouts, heart rate, HRV, active calories
  • Health Connect (Android): Steps, sleep, workouts, heart rate

You control which data types we access through device permissions.

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data:

  • Device information (model, operating system, app version)
  • IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
  • Log data (timestamps, errors, crashes)
  • Analytics events (features used, screens viewed)

We use analytics to improve the app, not for advertising.

1.4 Camera Permission

We request camera access only to capture progress photos, profile images, or scan food barcodes when you choose to use these features. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

1.5 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Precise geolocation (unless explicitly needed for a feature you enable)
  • Access to your contacts
  • Microphone access
  • Social media data beyond basic profile info for Apple/Google sign-in

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide app features (logging, insights, reminders)
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To improve the app's performance and reliability
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We share data only with trusted service providers as needed to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication, database, storage, analytics
  • RevenueCat - Subscription management
  • OpenFoodFacts - Nutrition database (barcode scanning)

All providers are bound by data processing agreements and confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Retention

  • We retain data while your account is active
  • We delete your data within 30 days of account deletion
  • Some data may be retained longer if required by law

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security reviews

No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

6. Your Rights Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Data portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@grandpoint.uk.

You can also export your data directly in the app: Settings > Data Export.

7. Children's Privacy

Kinesa is intended for users 16 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Some service providers may process data outside the UK/EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

9. Community Challenges Data

9.1 Additional Data in Challenges

When you participate in community features, we collect:

  • Challenge participation records
  • Progress metrics shared with participants (if you opt in)
  • Rankings and leaderboard positions (if you opt in)
  • Posts, comments, and reactions
  • Display name and profile photo

9.2 Who Sees Your Challenge Data

Other participants can see:

  • Your display name and avatar
  • Your progress metrics (if you opt in)
  • Your ranking (if you opt in)
  • Your posts and comments

We never share your email, exact location, or full health data with other participants.

9.3 Your Choices

  • Use a display name different from your real name
  • Opt out of rankings at any time
  • Leave challenges at any time
  • Delete your posts before leaving
  • Disable community features entirely in Settings

10. Cookies and Tracking

Kinesa is a native mobile app and does not use cookies. Analytics SDKs may store small amounts of data locally for usage tracking.

11. Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails at any time. Transactional emails (account confirmations, subscription notices) are required for service operation.

12. Third-Party Links

Kinesa may link to third-party services. Their privacy policies apply when you use those services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
