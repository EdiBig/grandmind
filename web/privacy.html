Kinesa Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Kinesa helps you track workouts, habits, nutrition, and wellbeing. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

Data We Collect

  • Account details (email, display name, profile photo).
  • Workout, habit, nutrition, and wellness logs you create.
  • Progress photos you upload (optional).
  • Device and usage data for reliability and analytics.

Camera Permission

We request camera access only to capture progress photos or profile images when you choose to take a photo inside the app. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide app features such as logging, reminders, and insights.
  • To sync across devices and back up your data.
  • To improve product stability and performance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted service providers to operate the app (for example, cloud storage or analytics), under strict confidentiality agreements.

Data Retention

Your data is stored while your account is active. You can delete your account in Settings to remove your data.

Contact

If you have questions or requests, contact us at info@grandpoint.uk or support@grandpoint.uk.
