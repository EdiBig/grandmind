Kinesa Account Deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your Kinesa account and associated data.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at support@grandpoint.uk.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address you used to sign in to Kinesa.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile (email, display name, profile photo).
  • Workout logs, habit data, nutrition logs, and wellness check-ins.
  • Progress photos stored in Kinesa.

What we may retain

  • Minimal transactional or legal records required for compliance.
  • Aggregated analytics that do not identify you personally.

Retention period

We process deletion requests within 30 days. Any required legal records are retained only as long as necessary to meet compliance obligations.
