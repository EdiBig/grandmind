# Kinesa Help Centre

Welcome to the Kinesa Help Centre. Find answers to common questions below, or
contact us at support@grandpoint.uk.

## Getting Started

How do I create an account?
1. Download Kinesa from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android)
2. Tap "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and create a password (or sign in with Apple/Google)
4. Complete the onboarding to customize your experience

What should I track?
During setup, you will choose which modules to enable:
- Workouts: Log exercises, sets, reps, or quick log
- Habits: Track daily habits (water, steps, meditation)
- Mood/Energy: Daily check-ins to understand patterns
- Nutrition: Simple meal quality tracking
- Sleep: Manual entry or import from Apple Health/Google Fit

You can change these anytime in Settings -> Modules.

What is the difference between Clinical and Coach tone?
- Clinical: Data-focused, neutral language
- Coach: Encouraging, personal, supportive tone

Choose what feels right for you. You can switch in Settings -> Preferences -> Tone.

## Using Kinesa

How do I log a workout?
1. Tap the + button on your Dashboard
2. Select Workout
3. Choose workout type (Strength, Cardio, Yoga, etc.)
4. Quick log: tap I worked out today
5. Detailed log: add exercises, sets, reps, weight
6. Optional: rate your energy before/after and add notes

How do streaks work?
Kinesa uses adaptive streaks designed for real life:
- Set a weekly goal (for example, 4 workouts per week)
- Your streak continues as long as you hit your weekly target
- Missing one day does not break your streak
- You can adjust your goal anytime in Settings -> Goals

Can I edit or delete logged data?
Yes. Tap any logged item (workout, habit, mood) to edit or delete it.

How do I check in my energy level?
1. Open the app
2. On your Dashboard, tap How is your energy today?
3. Select 1-5 (1 = exhausted, 5 = great)
4. Optional: add context tags

The app may suggest adjusting your workout based on your energy.

## Apple Health and Wearables

How do I connect Apple Health?
1. Go to Settings -> Integrations -> Apple Health
2. Tap Connect
3. Grant permissions (Steps, Sleep, Workouts, Heart Rate)
4. Data syncs automatically once daily

What data does Kinesa read from Apple Health?
- Steps (daily count)
- Sleep (hours and quality)
- Workouts (type, duration, calories)
- Heart Rate and HRV (for Premium recovery insights)

What data does Kinesa write to Apple Health?
- Workouts you log in Kinesa

Can I disconnect Apple Health?
Yes. Settings -> Integrations -> Apple Health -> Disconnect.

Do I need Apple Health or a wearable to use Kinesa?
No. Kinesa works great with manual logging. Wearables are optional.

## Premium Subscription

What is included in Free vs Premium?
Free:
- Unlimited workout logging (basic)
- 3 habits max
- Daily energy check-in
- Weekly summary (basic)
- Apple Health integration

Premium (GBP 4.99/mo or GBP 39.99/year):
- Unlimited habits (up to 10)
- Detailed exercise logging (sets/reps/weight)
- Advanced insights (patterns, correlations)
- Workout templates (PPL, Upper/Lower, etc.)
- Progress photos
- Data export (CSV)
- Priority support

How do I upgrade to Premium?
1. Go to Settings -> Subscription
2. Tap Upgrade to Premium
3. Choose Monthly (GBP 4.99) or Annual (GBP 39.99)
4. Complete purchase via App Store or Google Play

Is there a free trial?
Yes. Premium includes a 14-day free trial. Cancel anytime before the trial ends
and you will not be charged.

How do I cancel my subscription?
iOS: Settings app -> Subscriptions -> Kinesa -> Cancel
Android: Google Play -> Subscriptions -> Kinesa -> Cancel

Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple or Google per their policies. Contact Apple Support
or Google Play Support directly. Email us first at support@grandpoint.uk.

## Privacy and Data

What data does Kinesa collect?
Account info (email, name if provided), fitness data you log, wearable data you
choose to sync, and anonymized usage analytics.

Do you sell my data?
No. We monetize via subscriptions, not data sales.

Can I export my data?
Yes. Settings -> Data and Privacy -> Export My Data.

Can I delete my account?
Yes. Settings -> Data and Privacy -> Delete Account.

Is my data encrypted?
Yes. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

Where is my data stored?
Your data is stored on secure servers in the UK/EU. We comply with UK GDPR and
EU GDPR.

## Troubleshooting

The app will not sync Apple Health data:
1. Check permissions: iPhone Settings -> Privacy -> Health -> Kinesa
2. Force sync: Settings -> Integrations -> Apple Health -> Sync Now
3. Restart your iPhone
4. If still not working, email support@grandpoint.uk

I am not receiving notifications:
1. Check device settings -> Notifications -> Kinesa
2. Check in-app: Settings -> Notifications
3. Try logging out and back in

The app is slow or crashing:
1. Update to the latest version
2. Restart the app
3. Restart your phone
4. If it persists, email support@grandpoint.uk with device model and OS version

I forgot my password:
On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and follow the instructions.
